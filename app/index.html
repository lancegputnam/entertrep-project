EnterTrep

EnterTrep is dedicated to providing new and seasoned entrepreneurs with manufacturers, material suppliers, freelance designers and developers, industry research and mentors. Our mission is to create a community database for entrepreneurs where resources are shared amongst each other.

In Depth Profilew

EnterTrep includes free user profiles for entrepreneurs. Each profile includes connections, an about page, profile photo and links to your connections and favorite businesses. Each profile also comes with an "Idea Pad," which can be set to public or private. The Idea Pad will allow you to post images, create an inspiration board and create notes on your business.
